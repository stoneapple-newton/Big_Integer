# Big_Integer
This is a big integer file.
"bigint.hpp" allow you to + , - , * , / , % , = , == , != , <= , >= , < , > , += , -= , *= , /= , %= from a Wint to a Wint.like:
Wint(1)+Wint(2) 3
Wint(2)-Wint(1) 1
Wint(1)*Wint(2) 2
Wint(2)/Wint(1) 2
Wint(3)%Wint(2) 1
Wint(2)!=Wint(1)
Wint(1)<=Wint(2)
...
Just like int to use Wint.
